Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is Lifedots?
    Lifedots is a minimalist life-tracking app that visualizes your remaining days based on your birthday and life expectancy. It helps you live more intentionally and prioritize the time you have left.
  • How do I update my birthday or life expectancy?
    You can update your personal information in the Settings screen. Tap “My Profile” and adjust your birthday or expected lifespan. Changes will be reflected immediately in your visualization.
  • How is the remaining time calculated?
    We calculate your estimated remaining days based on your birth date and the life expectancy you provided. The visualization is updated daily to reflect the countdown.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes. Lifedots stores all your data securely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our top priority.
  • Does Lifedots support iOS widgets?
    Yes! Lifedots provides a home screen widget that displays your remaining days. To activate it, add the “Lifedots Countdown” widget via your home screen customization.

Troubleshooting

  • The widget is not updating automatically. What can I do?
    Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version and that widget permissions are granted. You can also open the app to trigger a refresh. Make sure background app refresh is enabled in your system settings.
  • I accidentally entered the wrong birthday. How do I reset it?
    Go to Settings > My Profile and update your birth date. The app will recalculate your remaining days automatically based on the new date.
  • I deleted the app. Will my data be restored when I reinstall?
    Currently, Lifedots data is stored locally. If you delete the app, your data will be lost. We recommend taking a screenshot or backing up important entries manually for now. Cloud sync is coming soon.
